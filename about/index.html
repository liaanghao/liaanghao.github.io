Brief Bio

Hao Liang is currently a Masters student majoring Sociology in Hitotsubashi University, advised by Prof. Takashi Machimura. Hao Liang is interested in Urban Sociology, Social Networks, Immigration, Social Ineuqality, Transnationailsm, Ethnicity, and East Asia. His master thesis focuses on how and why space matters for Chinese immigrants living in suburban Tokyo.
